About Our Project

An indoor campus navigation system developed using Agile Scrum methodology.

Who We Are

We are a student development team working collaboratively to design and implement a web-based indoor navigation system for ICT University. Our goal is to help new students, staff, and visitors easily locate buildings, classrooms, and key facilities on campus.

Our Approach

Hello

This project is developed using the Scrum framework. We organize our work into sprints, maintain a product backlog, and collaborate through continuous integration, peer reviews, and incremental improvements. This ensures quality, transparency, and teamwork throughout development.
