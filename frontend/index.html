ICTU Indoor Navigation System

ICT University is a leading institution focused on information and communication technologies, innovation, and practical learning.

▶ What This Application Does

This web-based indoor navigation system helps users locate classrooms, offices, and key facilities within ICT University.

▶ For Who It Is For

The system is designed for students, staff, and visitors who need quick and clear directions within the campus buildings.

▶ How It Helps

By providing an interactive map, the system reduces confusion, saves time, and improves the overall campus experience.
